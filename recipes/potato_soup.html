5 Can Potato Soup

Description

Cheap, easy, and filled with flavour. Who says that healthy food can't be cheap?

Ingredients

  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can chicken broth
  • 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed Cheddar cheese soup
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 (15 ounce) can diced potatoes, drained
  • Chives to garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk all cans except the diced potatoes in a saucepan on medium heat until well combined. Add potatoes, cook until hot, about 5 - 10 minutes.
