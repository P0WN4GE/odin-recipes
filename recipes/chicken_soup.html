No Water Chicken Soup

Description

Chicken soup that doesn't even require water? This recipe is amazing not only in its ease but also its flavour!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 1 leek, diced
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 ribs celery, diced
  • 8 button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 large apple, peeled and diced
  • 1 head green cabbage, cored and roughly chopped
  • 1 (4 to 5 pound) raw whole chicken
  • 6 thin slices ginger root

Steps

  1. Add olive oil to heavy bottom Dutch oven with a tight lid. Turn heat to medium-high, and add onions, leeks and salt. Cook for 10 minutes, ensuring any condensation drips back into the pot.
  2. Add celery, mushroom, apple, and cabbage. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  3. Add chicken and ginger. Cover and reduce heat to low. Simmer gently until chicken is cooked through, about 1 1/2 hours.
