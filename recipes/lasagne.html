Lasagne

Description

Succulent, flavorful, and perfect for a cozy weekend meal with the family, this classical dish never fails to disappoint no matter the time of year, or the occasion. Just be ready to be fighting for second servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Stir in curshed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, parsley, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add noodles and cook according to package directions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  4. In a large bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and salt to taste. Stir to combine.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. To assemble, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise across the sauce. Top with 1/3 of the mozarella cheese slices and some parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat layers of sauce, noodles, mozarella cheese, and parmesan cheese. Top with remaining parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let cool for 15 minutes before serving
